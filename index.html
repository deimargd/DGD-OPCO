FAQS

FAQs Questions

1. What financial services do you offer?

We offer financial project modeling, analytics, comprehensive reporting, CFO leadership, and strategic financial planning.

2. How can you help my business?

We provide tailored financial solutions to empower your business for success, including financial analysis, strategic planning, and leadership guidance. Our services are scalable to your needs, ensuring flexibility as your business evolves.

3. What industries do you specialize in?

Our expertise spans Real Estate, Software, Business Services, and more, allowing us to address diverse industry challenges.

4. What is your pricing structure?

Our transparent pricing includes a standard rate of $60 per hour, with fixed fees available for specific services. We offer flexible payment options and can bill directly from the US.

5. How do I get started?

Reach out via email, phone, or our website's contact form to schedule a free consultation. During the consultation, we'll create a customized plan to meet your needs. As a virtual CFO, we provide the advantage of scalability, accessibility, and expertise tailored to your business, all from the convenience of your location.

6. Do you offer a referral program?

Yes, we do! Ask about our referral program to learn how you can earn benefits when you refer new clients to us.

7. Is your company based in the US?

Yes, our company is based in the US, allowing us to provide seamless financial services directly to our clients.
